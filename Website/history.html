Who We Are

We are a 501c3 non-profit organization formed by concerned residents and conservationists of the Valley in
2002. We aim to protect the valley's history, agricultural heritage and natural resources. Over the years
of operation our organization has found value and purpose in both conservation, to preservation work, and even
community outreach projects. All of this for the central goal of maintaining this valley the wonderful life present in it.


Our Goals

1. To improve connections and communications with homeowners, landowners,
and the community in general regarding conservation and land preservation.

2. To protect open space and environmentally sensitive areas.

3. To strengthen our Board to prepare future leaders.

4. To provide support to and community involvement in the Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge.


Our Mission Statement

The Friends of Cherry Valley National Wildlife Refuge is dedicated to the conservation and enhancement
of the natural and cultural resources, rural character and scenic beauty of the Cherry Valley area.
